Waffle S'more Sundae

Back

Ingredients










Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Place frozen waffles
    on tray. Layer each with crushed chocolate
    graham crackers, chocolate chips, and mini
    marshmallows.
  2. Place topped waffles in oven. Bake for 10
    minutes or until crispy/marshmallow is
    melted.
  3. Remove warm waffle from oven and put in a
    bowl. Top with 3 scoops of chocolate ice
    cream.
  4. Top with peanuts, crushed graham cracker,
    chocolate syrup, caramel sauce, and whipped
    cream. Serve warm and enjoy!

Servings: 2

Cook time:10 min Prep time: 10 min

Cook Temperature: 400°F
