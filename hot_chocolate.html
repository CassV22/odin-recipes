Molten Hot Chocolate

Back

Ingredients








Instructions

  1. Mix milk, heavy cream, and chocolate bark in
    a saucepan over the stove top on medium
    heat.
  2. Stir until chocolate is completely melted and
    smooth.
  3. Pour melted chocolate into your favorite mug.
    Top with mini marshmallows, chocolate
    syrup and your favorite chocolate toppings
    (shavings, m&ms, cookie pieces, etc.).
  4. Top with peanuts, crushed graham cracker,
    chocolate syrup, caramel sauce, and whipped
    cream. Serve warm and enjoy!

Servings: 1

Cook time:10 min Prep time: 5 min

Cook Temperature: N/A
