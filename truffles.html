Instructions

  1. Combine chocolate bark and heavy cream in a
    saucepan over stove top. Stir over medium-
    high heat until chocolate is melted and
    smooth.
  2. Add butter and vanilla extract to mixture.
    Stir until well combined.
  3. Turn off heat and pour mixture into a glass
    bowl. Place bowl in fridge or freezer and let
    mixture chill until semi-hardened.
  4. Remove chilled mixture from fridge or
    freezer. Use small scooper to scoop out
    mixture and roll into balls. Place balls on
    nonstick tray.
  5. Garnish and roll balls in your favorite
    toppings. Place back in refrigerator for
    another 10 to 30 minutes to set.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

Servings: 10

Cook time: 4 min Prep time: 1 hr

Cook Temperature: N/A
