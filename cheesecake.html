Brownie Bottom Cheesecake

Back

Ingredients










Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. User circular shape
    cookie cutter to cut brownie crust pieces.
    Place pieces on nonstick tray and wrap in
    molds.
  2. Combine softened cream cheese, cocoa
    powder, and whipped cream in bowl. Mix
    thoroughly. Once smooth, slowly mix in eggs,
    sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Pipe cream cheese mixture into molds on top
    of brownie. Bake at 325°F for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove tray from oven and chill cakes for 1
    hour in refrigerator.
  5. Once chilled, remove tray from fridge and
    unwrap the molds. Garnish cakes with
    chocolate syrup (optional) and cocoa shavings.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

Servings: 10

Cook time: 20 min Prep time: 40 min

Cook Temperature: 325°F
