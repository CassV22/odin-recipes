Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine the dry
    ingredients in a bowl: flour, cocoa powder,
    baking powder, salt.
  2. Combine the wet ingredients and sugar in a
    separate bowl: eggs, vanilla, vegetable oil,
    milk, sugars. Mix thoroughly.
  3. While mixing, slowly pour the dry
    ingredients mix into the wet ingredient bowl.
    Mix until batter is smooth.
  4. Pour batter into two 8-inch nonstick cake
    pans. Put into the oven and bake for about 30 minutes.
    Check for completion with toothpick.
  5. When a toothpick comes out clean from the
    center, remove pans from oven and let cool for
    at least 45 minutes (place pans in fridge for
    faster cooling).
  6. Start buttercream while cake is cooling. In a
    mixing bowl, whip the butter, then slowly add
    the sugar, cocoa powder, and salt. Add the
    caramel sauce once the mix starts to become
    smooth and chocolatey.
  7. Whip buttercream mixture until smooth.
    Chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove everything from fridge. Take cooled
    cakes out of pans and place one lay on a plate.
    Cover center of layer with 1 cup of caramel
    sauce. Then top with a thin layer of the
    buttercream frosting.

Servings: 10

Cook time: 30 min Prep time: 30 min

Cook Temperature: 350°F
